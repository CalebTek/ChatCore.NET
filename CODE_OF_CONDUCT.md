# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ChatCore.NET is committed to providing a welcoming, inspiring, and harassment-free community for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, profession, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, and technical ability.

We expect all members of our community to be respectful, inclusive, and constructive in their interactions.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being Respectful** - Treat all members with dignity and respect
- **Being Inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds
- **Being Collaborative** - Work together toward common goals
- **Being Constructive** - Provide thoughtful, helpful feedback
- **Being Professional** - Maintain professionalism in all communications
- **Listening Actively** - Take time to understand others' perspectives
- **Assuming Good Intent** - Give people the benefit of the doubt
- **Being Patient** - Remember that everyone is learning and growing

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Discriminatory language or jokes
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Personal attacks or name-calling
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any form of violence or threats
- Spamming or abuse
- Exclusionary behavior based on any characteristic

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums, etc.)
- Official project events and conferences
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Private communications related to project business
- Any other conduct that violates this Code of Conduct

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a Code of Conduct violation:

1. **Document the incident** - Note the details, people involved, and context
2. **Contact maintainers** - Email calebinfotek@gmail.com with details
3. **Provide context** - Include screenshots, logs, or other evidence if available
4. **Maintain confidentiality** - Your report will be handled privately

### Investigation Process

- Maintainers will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- The incident will be investigated promptly and fairly
- Involved parties will be interviewed
- A resolution will be determined based on findings
- All parties will be informed of the outcome

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

- **Warning** - Formal notice to cease behavior
- **Temporary Ban** - Removal from community for a set period
- **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from community
- **Legal Action** - In severe cases (harassment, threats, etc.)

---

## Conflict Resolution

We encourage addressing issues directly and respectfully:

1. **Communicate** - If comfortable, speak directly with the person involved
2. **Focus on impact** - Explain how their behavior affected you
3. **Seek understanding** - Listen to their perspective
4. **Find resolution** - Work together on a solution
5. **Follow up** - Ensure the issue has been resolved

If direct resolution isn't possible or appropriate, contact maintainers.
